Stormo learns about your store, your products, and your customers and then gives you one focused marketing action every day to help you get more customers without relying on paid ads from day one.  How is Stormo different from other marketing tools?  Most marketing tools give you data, dashboards, or templates and leave you to figure out what to do next. Stormo tells you exactly what to do, when to do it, and why. It works like a real marketing manager sitting beside you every day, not a tool you have to learn how to use. There are no complicated setups, no marketing degrees required, and no ad budgets needed to get started.  Do I need a marketing background to use Stormo?  Not at all. Stormo was built for store owners, not marketers. Whether you have never done marketing before or have tried a few things without success, Stormo meets you where you are and gives you clear, simple actions anyone can follow.  What platforms does Stormo work with?  Stormo works with all major ecommerce platforms including Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce, Squarespace, Wix, and BigCommerce. If you sell online, Stormo can help you get more customers.  Will Stormo work for my niche?  Yes. Stormo builds a personalized plan based on your specific store, your products, and your target customer. Do I need a big budget to use Stormo?  No. Stormo's Starter plan is built entirely around organic growth, meaning strategies that get you customers without spending money on ads.
